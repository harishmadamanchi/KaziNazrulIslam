Kazi Nazrul Islam
The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is espeically recognised as Bidrohi kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revolutio of that period and the British government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity, and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his carrer, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels, and essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghaxal. Nazrul wrote and composed nearly 4,000 songs (many recorded on HMV and gramphone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount." - Kazi Nazrul Islam
